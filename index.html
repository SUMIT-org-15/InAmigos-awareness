About the NGO

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social impact through education, environmental conservation, women empowerment, animal welfare, and community development. The foundation believes in inspiring people to work together for a better society through various social initiatives.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project BachpanShala

Providing quality education and learning opportunities to underprivileged children.

Project Prakriti

Promoting tree plantation and environmental awareness to build a greener future.

Project Udaan

Supporting women through education, skill development and empowerment programs.

Project Jeev

Caring for stray animals through rescue, food distribution, and welfare initiatives.
